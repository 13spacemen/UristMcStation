Chinsky updated:

  • Cryobags, cryo and general stasis changes. Main point is that stasis is no longer binary on/off, it doesn't stop life ticking, just slows it down (usually a lot). It chemical processing is slowed down too, so sometimes bigger stasis is not better.
  • Cryo. Being very cold now provides stasis on its own - and cryo is easiest way of doing it. At 80K, it applies 20x stasis factor (life ticks once in 40 seconds instead once in 2).
  • Cryo chemless healing was slowed down a bit. It also no longer magically multiplies chems in beakers by 10x. Cryox/clonex adjusted to work at same efficiency with this change.
